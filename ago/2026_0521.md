# 实验结果 2026-05-21

## 方法概述

**Our Method (CellFlow + GO Graph + Change Prediction)**:
- GO图传播 + 邻域attention (k=2 hops, max 128 neighbors)
- FiLM conditioning (替换concatenation)
- Change prediction head: 预测哪些基因会变化，BCE loss (weight=0.1)
- Velocity modulation: velocity *= change_prob
- 删除expression-side graph encoder

**CellFlow Baseline**:
- 原始CellFlow，PCA 50维，无图编码，无combined loss
